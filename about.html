About Destiny Estates Sober Living

Destiny Estates Sober Living, owned and operated by good, gentle men in recovery, boasts a collective experience of 32 years in the realm of alcoholism and addiction. Having traversed similar paths before finding sobriety, we empathize with our residents, offering a distinctive journey guided by 12-step meetings and fostered within a family-like atmosphere.

Nestled in a serene neighborhood, just three blocks from the bustling 69 South, which teems with Jacksonville's abundant employment opportunities, our luxurious home provides round-the-clock onsite management. Situated a mere two blocks away from a beautiful local park, the estate is set upon a sprawling 1.5-acre expanse with ample parking and a private, fenced-in yard featuring a shimmering pool.

Spanning 3500 square feet, our home consists of six bedrooms, three baths, and two living areas, including four furnished two-man rooms and one four-man entry suite. Complimentary Wi-Fi is provided for our residents' convenience.

Our commitment to fostering a serene atmosphere is upheld through regular mandatory house meetings, dedicated conflict resolution processes, and a strict zero-tolerance policy against drug or alcohol use. Additionally, we maintain a firm stance against any form of sexual misconduct, prior sexual offenses, violence, or threats of violence.

At Destiny Estates Sober Living, we pledge to provide a nurturing environment where residents can embark on their journey in recovery with dignity, respect, and unwavering support. Ready to take the next step? Get in touch with us now and discover how Destiny Estates can be your home for recovery!
